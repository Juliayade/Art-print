Presentation

Artistic approach

My work is part of a colorful and dreamlike universe where nature occupies a central place. I explore shapes and colors inspired by travel and everyday life, seeking to create a balance between realism and imagination. Through dry pastel, I express the vitality and sensuality of exotic plants and natural landscapes, in a contemporary and surrealist approach. My works are distinguished by a bright and contrasting palette and a structured composition. Each work is an invitation to a change of scenery, to reverie and contemplation of the living.

Discipline: Soft Pastel

Style: Contemporary art surrealisme, poetic and naturalistic realism

Topics: Trips, nature, exotic landscapes, tropical vegetation

Contact: julia@lapuyade.fr Tiktok : @juju_draw_

Associations: Active menberof the Art du Pastel en Normandie organization

Technic: Soft Pastel, Work on the light, saturation and materiality to achieve a vibrant, immersive rendering

An approach combining naturalistic realism, contemporary composition, and a surreal atmosphere
